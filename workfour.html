introduce
Located in ningnan new district, yuhuatai district, nanjing city, jiangsu province, nanjing science and technology museum covers an area of about 300,000 square meters, with a building area of 30,000 square meters and a total investment of 350 million yuan.
INanjing science and technology museum includes the main pavilion, science and technology cinema and other related supporting facilities. The main hall consists of permanent exhibition hall, non-permanent exhibition hall and international conference and communication hall. Among them, the permanent exhibition hall is divided into six exhibition areas according to the theme: exploration of the universe, diversity of the earth, intelligent host, creation of heaven and earth, ocean of knowledge and children's science and technology park. There are more than 350 exhibits in total, more than 70% of which are participatory projects.
