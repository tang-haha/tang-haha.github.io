introduce
Jingdong logistics is affiliated to jingdong group. With the mission of creating a logistics performance platform with the best customer experience, jingdong logistics promotes the transformation of consumption patterns and the improvement of social supply chain efficiency through open and intelligent strategic measures, organically combines logistics, business flow, capital flow and information flow, and realizes mutual trust and win-win with customers.
Jd logistics provides integrated logistics solutions for merchants through the layout of the national self-built warehouse distribution logistics network, realizing the sharing of inventory and the integrated processing of orders, and providing multiple services such as warehouse distribution integration, express delivery, cold chain, bulk delivery and logistics cloud.
