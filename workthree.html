Product rendering 产品渲染

Basic Info
Type: Picture
Tool: PS
Team member: 1
introduce
In product rendering, we take the object we want to show as the starting point of the space, and take it as the center. The three-dimensional sense of the up, down, left and right, front and rear of the picture becomes the difference of space that we need to understand in rendering. The "front, middle and back" scene that we often point out in our photography is also to show the spatial difference of the picture and make a picture more three-dimensional.
